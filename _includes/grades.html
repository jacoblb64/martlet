### Sorting Grades Grades can be sorted by course or grade. To sort grades, pass either `course` or `grade` to the `--sort` (or `-s`) option.
Terminal 
$ martlet grades --sort course
Fetching grades...
Authenticating...
CCOM 206: A
CHEM 120: B+
...
$ martlet grades --sort grade
Fetching grades...
Authenticating...
COMP 206: A
COMP 250: A
...
